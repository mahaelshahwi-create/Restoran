# Restoran Project Strategy

Welcome to the design strategy for our restaurant website. Here is the vision for each section:

### 1. Hero Section: "Sell the Experience"
*   **Goal:** Don’t just state facts; invite the customer into your world.
*   **Headline:** Replace generic text with: "Where Authentic Flavors Meet Culinary Art."

### 2. About Us: "The Secret is in the Story"
*   **Goal:** People love stories. Avoid dry language; write with passion.
*   **Approach:** Instead of just "Established in 2010," say: "Since 2010, we have believed that the finest ingredients are the secret to happiness in every dish we serve."

### 3. Menu: "Make Them Hungry at First Sight"
*   **Goal:** Use sensory language.
*   **Example:** Instead of just "Steak," use: "Slow-grilled prime beef, infused with our secret herb-butter blend, served with crispy, golden hand-cut fries."

### 4. Meet the Chefs: "Put a Face to the Craft"
*   **Goal:** Humanize your brand.
*   **Tip:** Use real, professional photos and add a "Chef’s Favorite Dish" note for each team member.

### 5. Testimonials: "Social Proof is Powerful"
*   **Goal:** Build trust.
*   **Example:** "Beyond just the food, this is my absolute favorite place to celebrate special moments!" — Sarah M.

### 6. Reservation: "Keep it Warm and Inviting"
*   **Goal:** Create a personal connection.
*   **Copy:** "We look forward to hosting you! Celebrating a special occasion? Let us know so we can make it extra memorable."
